/ About

25 Years of
Urban Development Impact

HigherX is a professional architecture company delivering large-scale urban and institutional projects across India and the Middle East. Akthar Iman is the Principal Architect of HigherX, leading a multidisciplinary studio focused on high-rise residential, commercial buildings, and villa communities.

The practice combines design innovation with engineering rigor, delivering integrated campuses, master-planned townships, and people-first urban environments with measurable impact.

Design Cell Leadership

Founded and led the Design Cell at KGD (A Katerra Design Partner), driving interdisciplinary collaboration across 200+ architects and engineers to deliver design excellence at scale.

Design Competitions Committee

Chaired the Design Competitions Committee, curating and leading submissions for national and international architectural competitions — winning multiple recognitions.

Urban Design & Historic Restoration

Active member of the Urban Design & Historic Restoration Committee, shaping policy for sustainable urban development and heritage conservation.

Barrier-Free Design Research

Published research on 'Barrier-Free Design' (2017), advocating for inclusive, human-centric architecture aligned with modern ESG and accessibility standards.
